The Choir

The CK Singers are a non-profit organization whose mandate is to serve Chatham-Kent. Our group sings concerts for Christmas- late November through early December as well as a spring line-up in late April and early May. We sing for free for nursing homes, having sung at St. Andrewâ€™s Residence, Hudson manor Tilbury, the Oaks Wallaceburg, Villa Angela and many others. These concerts are usually about 30-45 minutes and we do 3 or 4 each season.


The Venue

First Presbyterian Church on Wellington St. In Chatham is currently allowing us to rehearse at no cost at their sanctuary and we are grateful for this. In return, we sing one major concert each season at this venue and the free will donations raised go to the charity of their choice. This concert is usually 60-90 minutes in length and also includes local soloists and instrumentalists and occasionally audience sing-a-longs.

Our Conductor

Nancy Tapley

Nancy has a degree in organ performance (ARCT) and is the president of the Chatham Centre of the Royal Canadian College of Organists. She and has enjoyed being a choir director for over 30 years and enjoys working with the varied repertoire of Broadway, Theatre, Pop, Rock and classical music.

Our Accompanist

Joy Anderson

Joy is an exceptional pianist. She has her ARCT in teaching from the Royal Conservatory of Music. She is a member of the Ontario Registered Music Teacher Association and teaches privately. She is the organist and choir director at St. Joseph Church in Chatham and at the St. Francis Xavier Church in Tilbury.
